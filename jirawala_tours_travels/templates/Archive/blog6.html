Digital Documentation Requirements

All interstate travelers must carry valid digital or physical copies of driving licenses, vehicle registration, and insurance documents. E-challans and digital payments are now mandatory at most toll plazas.

Updated Toll & Tax Policies

  • FASTag Mandatory: All vehicles must have functional FASTag for seamless toll payments.
  • GST on Rentals: Updated GST rates apply to interstate car rental services.
  • State Entry Permits: Some states require online registration for commercial vehicles.

Safety & Environmental Norms

New emission standards are being enforced at state borders. Vehicles older than 15 years may face restrictions in certain metropolitan areas. Mandatory safety equipment checks at highway patrol points.

COVID-19 Related Guidelines

While most restrictions have been lifted, travelers should stay updated on any state-specific health protocols, especially during festival seasons or health emergencies.

Staying Compliant

Keep all documents updated and easily accessible. Use official government apps for real-time updates on road conditions and regulations. Our rental service ensures all vehicles meet current compliance standards.
