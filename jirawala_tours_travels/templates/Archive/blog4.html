Perfect Weekend Getaways from Gujarat

Discover amazing weekend destinations within driving distance

  1. Home

  3. Perfect Weekend Getaways from Gujarat

Hill Stations & Nature Retreats

Escape to the cool hills of Mount Abu, Saputara, or Matheran. These destinations offer refreshing weather, scenic viewpoints, and peaceful environments perfect for a weekend recharge.

Heritage & Cultural Sites

  • Udaipur: City of Lakes with magnificent palaces and romantic boat rides.
  • Jodhpur: The Blue City with impressive forts and vibrant markets.
  • Ajanta & Ellora: Ancient cave temples showcasing incredible architecture.

Beach Destinations

Drive to Goa's pristine beaches, Diu's tranquil shores, or explore the coastal beauty of Daman. Perfect for sun, sand, and seafood.

Adventure & Wildlife

Visit Gir National Park for lion safaris, explore the Rann of Kutch during festival season, or enjoy water sports at Sardar Sarovar Dam.

Planning Your Weekend Trip

Book your rental car in advance, pack light, and plan your itinerary to make the most of your short getaway. Most destinations are 3-6 hours drive from major Gujarat cities.
