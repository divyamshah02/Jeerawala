Top 10 Must‑Visit Destinations from Ahmedabad

Inspiring road‑trip ideas within a day's drive

  1. Home
  2. Our Blogs
  3. Top 10 Must‑Visit Destinations from Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad’s central location makes it a perfect gateway to some of western India’s most captivating spots. Here are our top 10:

  1. Mount Abu (230 km) — Rajasthan’s only hill‑station, famed for Dilwara Temples and Nakki Lake.
  2. Udaipur (260 km) — Romantic ‘City of Lakes’ dotted with palaces and Aravalli backdrops.
  3. Gir National Park (350 km) — Home of the majestic Asiatic lion.
  4. Rann of Kutch (400 km) — Experience the surreal white salt desert, especially during Rann Utsav.
  5. Diu Island (370 km) — Sandy beaches, Portuguese fort and laid‑back vibe.
  6. Vadodara (110 km) — Laxmi Vilas Palace, Sayaji Gardens and vibrant Navratri celebrations.
  7. Somnath Temple (410 km) — One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, perched by the Arabian Sea.
  8. Statue of Unity (200 km) — World’s tallest statue honouring Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
  9. Porbandar (400 km) — Birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi, coastal charm included.
  10. Bhuj (330 km) — Gateway to Kutch crafts, palaces and deserts.

Plan a comfortable itinerary of two to three stops per day and pre‑book accommodations during peak season.
