How to Prepare Your Car for a Long Journey

A step‑by‑step maintenance checklist before you hit the road

  1. Home
  2. Our Blogs
  3. How to Prepare Your Car for a Long Journey

Pre‑Trip Inspection

Schedule a mechanic visit a week prior. Check belts, hoses, battery health and coolant system.

Fluid Top‑Up

  • Engine oil
  • Brake & clutch fluid
  • Power‑steering fluid
  • Windshield washer fluid

Tyre & Brake Care

Inflate to manufacturer PSI, examine tread depth and carry a functional spare. Replace worn brake pads to avoid fade.

Emergency Preparedness

Pack a flashlight, reflective triangle, jumper cables, basic tools and a comprehensive first‑aid kit.

On‑Road Etiquette

Maintain steady speeds, avoid aggressive acceleration and take 15‑minute breaks every two hours.
